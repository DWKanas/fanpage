Bio

Highly Suspect is an American rock band from Cape Cod, Massachusets. The band consists of twin brothers Rich and Ryan Meyer, Johnny Stevens, and Matt Kofos. They started as a bar cover band, but after relocating to Brooklyn, New York and recording their first album with producer Joel Hamilton things quickly started taking off. Leading to the successful band they are today
